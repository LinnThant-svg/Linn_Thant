About 100SMILES Dental Care

Three decades of excellence in dental care

Our Story

A modern dental care facility located at 33 Fantail Street in Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care has been serving the community for over three decades. Our roots in the community run deep, and our unwavering dedication is to provide the best possible dental care for our patients.

With over 50 years of combined experience in dental care, we pride ourselves on offering friendly, reliable, and compassionate services to patients of all ages. Whether it is a child's first dental visit or an adult's routine check-up, we care for everyone. Our patients come from all walks of life, from young children to retirees.

Our Promise

At 100SMILES Dental Care, our promise is to deliver the highest standard of care for our patients, regardless of their age or dental needs. Our dedicated team of dentists and support staff shares a profound commitment to dental health, ensuring that our patients receive the attention and treatment they deserve.

While we help with acute and emergency dental care, we also focus on fostering long-term relationships and providing preventative care like check-ups, cleanings, and patient education. We believe that informed patients make the best decisions about their dental health, which is why we prioritize education and communication at every visit.

Our experienced dentists are well-equipped to handle common dental issues, offering diagnostics, treatments, and surgeries when necessary.
